# MezTal Relume Master Prompt

**Role:** Expert Information Architect & Web Strategist.
**Goal:** Generate a comprehensive sitemap and wireframes for **MezTal.com**, a US-facing nearshore staffing partner.

## 1. Core Identity & Rules (Non-Negotiable)
*   **What:** MezTal builds dedicated, long-term remote teams in Mexico (not gig work).
*   **Locations:** Always "Guadalajara and Mexico City" (never just one).
*   **Time Zone:** Always "Overlapping business hours with the US" (NEVER "same time zone").
*   **Value:** ~40% cost savings, cultural alignment, "You make the hiring decisions, we manage the messy stuff (HR/Payroll/IT)."
*   **Strict Separation:** **Blog** (News/Culture) and **Resources** (Guides/Case Studies) must be separate.

## 2. Sitemap Structure (Hub -> Cluster -> Leaf)

### 1. Home
*   **Goal:** Conversion for cold traffic.
*   **Nav:** Minimal (Solutions, Industries, Why Mexico, Contact).
*   **Key Sections:** Hero (Value Prop), Social Proof (Logos), "How it Works", Service Clusters, Why Mexico, Testimonials, CTA.

### 2. Hire Talent (Solutions Hub)
*   **Clusters (Pillar Pages):**
    *   **Accounting & Finance** (Controllers, AP/AR, Payroll)
    *   **IT & Data Staffing** (Devs, Data Engineers, Help Desk)
    *   **Marketing & Creative** (Digital Marketers, Designers)
    *   **HR & Augmented Workforce** (Recruiters, Sourcers)
    *   **Operations & Admin** (Back-office, Executive Assistants)
    *   **Customer Support** (Call Center, CS Reps)
*   **Role Pages (Leafs):** Link to specific roles (e.g., "Hire Data Engineer", "Hire Controller").

### 3. Industries (Hub)
*   **Vertical Pillars:**
    *   **Senior Living & Post-Acute** (Primary Vertical)
    *   **AgeTech & Elder Care Tech**
    *   **Healthcare**
    *   **B2B SaaS / Technology**
    *   **Real Estate**
*   **Content:** Industry pain points, relevant roles, case study snippets.

### 4. Why Mexico (Pillar)
*   **Content:** Why Guadalajara + Mexico City, Talent quality, Cost savings (~40%), "Silicon Valley of LatAm".
*   **SEO Landers:** "Nearshore vs Offshore", "Staff Augmentation Mexico".

### 5. Resources (Hub)
*   **Types:**
    *   **Case Studies:** Success stories (Problem/Solution/Result).
    *   **Comparisons:** "Best IT Staffing Agencies", "Nearshore vs Offshore".
    *   **Guides:** "How to hire in Mexico", "US GAAP for Nearshore".
    *   **FAQ Library:** Cost, Control, Compliance.

### 6. Blog (Hub)
*   **Categories:** Industry News, Company Culture, Leadership Insights.
*   **Note:** Distinct from Resources.

### 7. Company (Hub)
*   **Pages:** About Us, Team (Leadership), Locations (GDL, CDMX, Houston), LinkedIn Hub (Social Feed).

### 8. Contact
*   **Forms:** Client Inquiry (Primary), Candidate Application (Secondary).

### 9. Careers (Candidate Hub)
*   **Focus:** Attracting talent in Mexico.
*   **Content:** Culture, Benefits, Open Roles (Workable Embed).

## 3. Navigation Strategy
*   **Variant A (Home):** Minimal. Logo, Solutions, Industries, Why Mexico, Contact. CTA: "Book a Consult".
*   **Variant B (Inner/SEO):** Full Context. Adds Resources, Blog, Company.
*   **Variant C (Careers):** Candidate-focused. Careers, About, Team, Contact. CTA: "View Open Roles".

## 4. Page Template Requirements
*   **Global Elements:** Sticky Nav (Variant based on page), Footer (Sitemap links + Socials).
*   **Conversion:** "Book a Consult" CTA prominent on all Buyer pages.
*   **Visuals:** Professional, clean, "Premium Nearshore" aesthetic.
