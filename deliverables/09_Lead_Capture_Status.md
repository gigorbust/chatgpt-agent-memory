## Executive Summary

Lead capture is the primary conversion goal of the website. We will implement high-converting forms integrated with HubSpot to ensure seamless lead management.

## Strategy

### 1. Form Types
*   **"Book a Consult" (Primary):** Short form (Name, Email, Company, Needs) located in the Hero and Sticky Header.
*   **"Download Resource" (Secondary):** Gated content form for Case Studies/Whitepapers.
*   **"Apply Now" (Candidate):** Separate workflow for job seekers, routed to Workable or HR email.

### 2. Integration
*   **Platform:** HubSpot Forms (embedded) or Wix Forms (synced via Zapier/Native integration).
*   **Routing:**
    *   Client Leads -> Sales Pipeline
    *   Candidate Leads -> Recruitment Pipeline

## Next Steps
*   Design form fields during the Wireframing phase.
*   Set up HubSpot integration during the Implementation phase.
